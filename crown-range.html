Crown Range Meats

The Crown Range brand focuses on bringing New Zealand ground beef & lamb to consumers in the United States. For this project, I created packaging and sales material with a very quick turnaround: less than one week! With the help of a very nimble team, the box underwent photography, design, and USDA approval in time to fill grocery store shelves before the 4th of July.
