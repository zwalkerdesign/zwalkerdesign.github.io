Ovation Chef-Ready Lamb

Ovation is a New Zealand lamb producer focused on supplying customers with high quality, expertly butchered portions. I was in charge of creating the U.S. marketing collateral, which accentuated the program's unique butchering & packaging system. For this project, I used striking product photos to highlight the quality of the lamb and emphasize its utility to chefs.
