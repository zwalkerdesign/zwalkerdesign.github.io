Zulily

Zulily.com is an e-commerce site that offers daily deals from major brands such as LEGO, Nike, and Le Creuset at marked-down prices. As the Visual Designer, I specialize in seasonal campaigns & special programs, as well as designing style guides, templates, and cross-channel asset suites.

Launching Zulily's integrated campigns requires communication across many different channels and stakeholders; from landing pages & emails, to social & ads, building a cohesive & branded experience is as fun as it is challenging!

Art Direction, Branding, Typography, Illustration, Digital Marketing

Birthday 2023

With only a month-long timeline from concepting to launch, I stuck with what I knew (who doesn't love balloons!?). This project was a fun exploration of illustration & brand colors, and opened the door to a wider discussion about where they fit in with the future of Zulily's brand.

Holiday 2022

One of Zulily's most profitable campaigns, Holiday 2022 decked the site in sparkles and gift wrap. The landing page below is meant to be users' one-stop-shop for all things holiday, and was the destination for all offsite ads & social.

Black Friday / Cyber Monday 2022

What's more exciting than a good deal?? In keeping with Holiday's "sparkle and gift wrap" design styling, we punched it up with the bright green & black combo, while using similar imagery to keep the site looking cohesive as it shifted from Cyber Monday into the full-swing Holiday look.

Savings Spree Program

Tasked with creating the look for Zulily's massive quarterly sale program, I ran with the underutilized orange & blue from Zulily's supportive brand colors. The bright color-blocks paired with a simple circle & large discount typopgraphy served to emphasize the sale's huge savings.
